Hersheys Chocolate Milkshake

Description

What would your favorite movie night be without a decadent treat. Ah, yes, we must have something sweet to was down that savory, buttery, salty popcorn that we just destroyed an unearthly amount of. But what shall it be?

Enter, the original Hersheys Chocolate Milkshake. Simple, yet delicious. Everybody will be happy with this one. And the best part is, there are only 3 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 4 scoops vanilla or chocolate ice cream
  • 1/2 cup cold milk
  • 1/4 cup HERSHEY'S Syrup

Steps

  1. Place ice cream, milk and syrup in blender container
  2. Cover; blend until smooth. Garnish, if desired. serve immediately. Two 10-oz servings
